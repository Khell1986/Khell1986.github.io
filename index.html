An Introduction

Hi, I’m Kim Hellmer, a solution based problem solver. I’ve always liked to fix stuff. I’m pretty good at taking something that’s damaged and making it functional again. I have a several years of experience in fixing people and twice that in troubleshooting with technology.  I use empathy and compassion to make real connections with people; personally going the extra mile to earn lifelong clients.

I’m a graduate of the YWEB Career Academy. I’m interested in continuing my education in back end web development. I’ve gotten my feet wet over the past few months. I’m ready to dive into SQL jQuery Python, & Ruby. I’m always looking for opportunity to learn more about technology or collaborate with others.

I spend about half of my free time volunteering. Currently, I am the West Madison lead for Box of Balloons, a non-profit that provides birthday parties for children in financial need. Each week, I organize the birthday requests from social workers, create a profile for each child, send it to our volunteers, arraign pick up of donated items, and ensure each box is filled with everything needed to have a wonderful birthday before delivering to the social worker. I use my creative side to make personalized party items for each birthday. This is a real passion project. I love helping to brighten the life of a child.
